RULES

1. The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.

2. 10 marks for correct answer and 5 negative marks for wrong answer.

3. The quizzes are all in English.

4. Anyone suspected by an official of cheating will be disqualified. You must not use any mobile device during a quiz competition.

5. Participation in the quiz is free and open to all persons above 18 years old.
